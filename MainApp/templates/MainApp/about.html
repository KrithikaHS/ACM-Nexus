ACM Nexus

With its roots in Sanskrit, the word "ABHIPRAJNA" means "Towards Greater Knowledge," encapsulating the ultimate purpose of scientific exploration. Paired with Accio Curiosite, which translates to "Summon your Curiosity," the event urges its participants to venture deeper into the pursuit of science.

TRADITIONS

Since its provenance in 2019, ABHIPRAJNA has been a dynamic platform for curious minds to come together and explore the vast world of science. What began as a coordinated effort to unite brilliant minds has now evolved into an annual tradition, with each edition offering a fresh and unique perspective.
